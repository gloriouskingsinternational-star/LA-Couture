Help Centre

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place a bespoke order?

Fill in the enquiry form, select "Bespoke Commission," and include details about your occasion and timeline. We'll contact you within 24 hours to schedule a consultation at our Azhata showroom or via video call.

What is your delivery timeline?

Ready-to-wear pieces are dispatched within 3–5 business days. Bespoke commissions typically take 4–8 weeks depending on complexity. We deliver nationwide across Nigeria and ship internationally.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Paystack, bank transfer, Visa, Mastercard, and Verve. A 50% deposit is required for all bespoke orders, with the balance due before dispatch.

Do you offer returns or exchanges?

Ready-to-wear items may be returned within 7 days of receipt, unworn and in original packaging. Bespoke and custom pieces are non-refundable. Please contact us on WhatsApp to initiate a return.

Can I visit the showroom in Abuja?

Yes! Our showroom is in Azhata, FCT Abuja. Visits are by appointment only. Book via WhatsApp or the enquiry form above to secure your preferred time slot.

Do you do Agbada and native wear?

Absolutely. Agbada, senator sets, Aso-Oke, and all forms of native wear are our speciality. We craft bespoke native wear with premium fabrics sourced locally and internationally.
